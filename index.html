Mission Statement

Recovery is Our Primary Purpose

We are a supportive recovery home committed to providing a strong and healthy environment. This house was begun and is led by fellow recovering addicts who understand how hard but rewarding it can be to take the next steps in recovery after treatment.

Resilience, courage and commitment will be the keystones as we continue to grow into responsible and successful women. Through mutual respect and understanding, we can build a beautifully healthy support system that can last a lifetime

At Tranquility House we believe our success is only measured by the success of our members.

“Behind every strong woman is a tribe of other strong women who have her back.”

  • Mandatory weekly house meetings
  • Random drug and alcohol testing
  • Required meeting attendance and actively working with a sponsor
  • Full time Resident House Manager
  • Assistance with necessary resources including rent assistance, food pantries and job placement
  • Life Skills and Mental Health Education available
  • High accountability expected for all members
  • Regular Unity Events

As a member of Tranquility House, you will have the freedom to focus on your individual recovery plan while being in a structured and supportive environment. Our House Manager, Shelly, has a Bachelor’s in Social Work from The University of Texas at Austin and has worked closely with the MHMR community. Shelly has worked at Austin State Hospital, Austin Travis County Integral Care and Williamson County Bluebonnet Trails with experience teaching Life Skills Programs, COPSD and other Mental Health Education.

She is a fellow recovering addict that shows incredible courage and strength in her recovery program while rebuilding the relationships with her children and family. Shelly has a passion and personal understanding of what it takes to continue along the path of sobriety.
